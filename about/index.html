About PKF Chartouni

Founded in 1985 and a member of the PKF International network since 1992, we combine local expertise with global reach to support your business growth.

Our Story

PKF Chartouni is a leading professional services firm in Lebanon, providing a comprehensive range of assurance, tax, and advisory services to a broad client base. With deep local roots and a strong understanding of the Lebanese business environment, we have built a reputation for technical excellence, integrity, and client-focused service.

Our capabilities are further strengthened through our membership in PKF International, a global network of legally independent firms, enabling us to combine local expertise with international standards, resources, and reach.

We serve clients across a wide range of industries, from family-owned and entrepreneurial businesses to large local and international organizations. By taking the time to understand each client’s specific objectives and challenges, we deliver tailored, practical solutions that create lasting value.
